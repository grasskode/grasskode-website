## postimg with lightbox

find:

<a data-flickr-embed="true"  href=\"[\S]*\" title="[\S]*"><img src="([\S]*).jpg" width="[\S]*" height="[\S]*" alt="([\S]*)"><\/a><script async src="[\S]*" charset="utf-8"><\/script>

replace:

<div class="postimg">
  <a href="$1_c.jpg" data-toggle="lightbox">
    <img src="$1" alt="$2">
  </a>
  <em>$2</em>
</div>
