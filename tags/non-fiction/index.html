John H. Hick - Philosophy of Religion

Philosophy of Religion by John Harwood Hick My rating: 3 of 5 stars I was advised about reading “Philosophy of Religion” by an uncle of mine as a response to another post. I had put it on my reading list and even got myself a copy but the dry schoolbook-like text had put me off the task of reading it for quite a while. Eventually I did get to it. The book starts of in a rather dry fashion, but you need to remember that this is primarily an academic text.
